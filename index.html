MMXXVI · A RELIC IN THE VOID

AETERNUM

Memento mori — remember that you must die.

descend
I

The Descent

Below the last light there is a stone that was never quarried. It turns in the dark, indifferent, older than the names we gave it.

We came down to look upon it. None of us came back unchanged.

II

The Relic

Its surface drinks the light and answers in embers. Fracture it, and it does not break — it remembers a shape from before the world.

  • forged before the first dawn
  • weight the sum of every silence
  • purpose unknown · feared · adored
III

The Reckoning

It opens. A furnace where a heart should be. For one held breath the void is the colour of blood and gold, and everything you were is weighed against nothing at all.

IV

Eternity

The shards return to stillness. The relic forgets you, as it forgets all things, and turns again in the dark — waiting for the next to descend.
